What is Airtap?

Airtap.ai is a revolutionary platform that allows your AI assistant to interact with apps in the cloud—just as you would—by tapping buttons, submitting forms, or triggering actions. No need to install apps on your device. No need to open anything. Airtap is your smart cloud interface that acts on your behalf.

How It Works

Cloud-Native App Hosting

Your apps live and run in the cloud, not on your phone. No installs, no updates, just access.

AI-Driven Interactions

Your AI agent simulates user behavior by tapping into interfaces—triggering actions like you would.

Context-Aware Automation

Airtap learns from your habits and automates daily routines: scheduling, reordering, reminders, and more.

Seamless API Integration

Connect with existing tools or custom systems. Airtap fits into your workflow easily.
